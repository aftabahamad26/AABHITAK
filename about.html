About

AABHI TAK is a customizable news aggregator that brings you top headlines and trending stories with a clean, responsive UI. You can search, filter by category, and personalize the theme, fonts, and layout.

Real-time headlines are fetched through our backend proxy to keep API keys secure, with intelligent enrichment to increase article volume and freshness, plus RSS fallbacks for reliability.
